About me

I am a broadly interested Berliner with a background in ecoomics and a passion for culture and languages. Having worked as a consultant and project-manager in digital as well as governmental projects I have been able to improve my organization and presentation skills. Also I know how to interact with different types of people and clients as I was dealing with colleagues, business industry, the public sector and also the educational sector in the projects I have worked on. Due to working very closely with developers I have acquired some insight on the world of coding and wanted to get a better understanding of it to be able to code myself. So I decided to attend a coding bootcamp to dive deeper into the world of Javascript and Co. and become a Full Stack Web Developer. The thing that strikes me the most about becoming a developer is the instant gratification you get when all the effort you put into your code is working. Now, after having successfully graduated I am keen on starting to work as a Web-Developer. Besides my new love for coding I recently discovered my love for plants. It gives me joy seeing them grow and flourish as I take care of them and it is also a kind of meditation for me.
